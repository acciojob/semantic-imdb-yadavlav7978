Summary

"3 Idiots" is a comedy-drama film directed by Rajkumar Hirani, based on the novel "Five Point Someone" by Chetan Bhagat. The story revolves around the friendship of three engineering students at an Indian engineering college.

Actors

The film stars Aamir Khan, R. Madhavan, and Sharman Joshi in the lead roles.

Quotes

One of the memorable quotes from the movie is: "All is well."
